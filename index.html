SEWN in the USA is a culmination of the founder, Candice Stewart's passion for well-constructed statement pieces, conscious and responsible production, and supporting local designers, makers and skilled laborers. SEWN caters to the need for a curated collection of lovely unique, yet essential pieces to a women's wardrobe exclusively made in the USA. The collection espouses a distinctly modern and urban aesthetic, and represents a "luxe-grit" style - refined, but a little rough around the edges.

Maptote

Maptote is the culmination of Brooklyn-based husband & wife duo: cartographer Michael Berick & stylist Rachel Rheingold. Together they created Maptote, a line of products decorated with maps of locales both domestic and exotic. Natural, sustainable cotton canvas totes and bags featuring playful graphics that embrace the places & functions they represent.
